### Welcome to India Daily Facts

**India Daily Facts** (`pishorkar.tech`) is an independent digital news and factual update platform dedicated to delivering objective, concise, and structured coverage of current affairs across India and the globe.

---

### Our Mission
In an era of clickbait and overwhelming news feeds, our goal is simple: deliver key facts quickly without sensationalism. Every article on our platform is structured with:
* **Quick Summaries** for readers on the go.
* **In-Depth Factual Analysis** providing essential context.
* **Key Takeaways** outlining the future implications of major news events.

---

### Editorial Integrity & Fact-Checking
We source our information from accredited public records, press releases, and national news channels. We strive for 100% neutrality, avoiding political bias or subjective commentary.
